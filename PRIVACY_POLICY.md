# Privacy Policy — ExtraAI

**Last updated:** 2026

## Introduction
ExtraAI ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI assistant application. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Information We Collect
- **Chat messages:** The text, questions, and prompts you send are processed to generate AI responses and may be stored in our database so you can revisit your conversation history.
- **Uploaded files:** Images and PDFs you upload are processed temporarily to answer your questions. PDF text is extracted in your browser.
- **No personal account required:** ExtraAI does not require you to create an account or provide personal identification.

## How We Use Information
- To generate AI responses to your queries.
- To save your conversation history for your convenience.
- To improve the quality and reliability of the service.

## Third-Party AI Services
ExtraAI sends your prompts to third-party AI providers (such as Pollinations, Groq, OpenAI, Google Gemini, or OpenRouter, depending on configuration) to generate responses. Please review their respective privacy policies. We do not sell your data.

## Data Storage
- Conversations are stored in a secure PostgreSQL database.
- You can delete any conversation at any time from within the app.

## Data Security
- API keys and secrets are stored server-side and never exposed to the browser.
- Communication uses HTTPS encryption.

## Children's Privacy
ExtraAI is intended for general audiences and is rated suitable for everyone. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Voice Input
Voice input uses your device/browser's built-in speech recognition. Audio is processed by your browser/device, not stored by us.

## Your Rights
- Delete conversations anytime.
- Stop using the app to stop all data processing.

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this URL.
